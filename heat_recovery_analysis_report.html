Heat Recovery Analysis Report

This report summarizes an analysis of hot and chilled water loops and the potential for heat recovery based on IESVE simulation results. It provides metrics, graphs, and other information useful in understanding the loads on these loops and the opportunities for simultaneous heating and cooling. With sufficient heat recovery potential, projects should consider the use of condenser water heat recovery, heat recovery chillers, central plant heat pumps, or other solutions. These are important decarbonization strategies to support electrification of heating systems and/or to reduce the demand on fossil fuel heating systems.
